NovaCRM Getting Started Guide

Step 1: Create Your Account
Visit app.novacrm.com/signup and enter your email address. You'll receive a verification email within 30 seconds. Click the link to activate your account and set your password. No credit card required for the 14-day free trial.

Step 2: Set Up Your Organization
After signing in, you'll be guided through initial setup:
- Company name and industry
- Team size and primary use case
- Timezone and currency preferences
- Logo upload (displayed in emails and chat widget)

Step 3: Import Your Data
NovaCRM supports importing contacts and deals from:
- CSV/Excel files (up to 500,000 rows per import)
- Salesforce (full migration tool with field mapping)
- HubSpot (full migration tool with field mapping)
- Pipedrive (contact and deal import)
- Google Contacts
- Outlook Contacts

The import wizard guides you through field mapping and deduplication. Large imports (>50,000 contacts) are processed in the background and typically complete within 30 minutes.

Step 4: Connect Your Email
Go to Settings > Email and connect your Gmail or Outlook account. This enables:
- Automatic email logging to contact records
- Send emails directly from NovaCRM
- Email open and click tracking
- Email templates and sequences

Step 5: Set Up Your Pipeline
Navigate to Deals > Pipeline Settings to customize your sales stages. Default stages are: Lead, Qualified, Proposal, Negotiation, Won, Lost. You can rename, add, or remove stages. Set probability percentages for revenue forecasting.

Step 6: Invite Your Team
Go to Settings > Team to invite colleagues. Assign roles (Admin, Manager, Sales Rep, Read-Only) to control access. Managers can see their team's activities and pipeline. Admins have full access to all settings.

Step 7: Install Integrations
Connect your essential tools from Settings > Integrations. Recommended first integrations: email provider, calendar, and Slack/Teams for notifications.

Step 8: Customize Your Workspace
- Create custom fields for contacts and deals
- Set up saved views and filters
- Configure notification preferences
- Set up workflow automations for common tasks

Need help? Book a free 30-minute onboarding call at novacrm.com/onboarding or contact support@novacrm.com.
